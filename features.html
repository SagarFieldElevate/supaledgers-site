Accounting

Production-grade books. Not a toy.

Full double-entry ledger with immutability. This isn't a simplified cash-basis tracker — it's real accounting software with a real chart of accounts, real journal entries, and real financial statements.

  • Double-entry ledger with immutable audit trail
  • Invoicing with online payment via Stripe
  • Bills and accounts payable with approval workflow
  • Check writing with check register
  • Bank transaction import + AI categorization
  • Trial balance, P&L, balance sheet, AR/AP aging
  • Recurring transactions and fiscal year closing

Scheduling & Dispatch

From calendar to completion. Automatically.

  • Drag-and-drop dispatch board

  • Mobile tech view with tap-to-navigate
  • Skill-based routing
  • Recurring service visits

Talk to your books. They'll listen.

Natural language voice commands. Say what you need, see what the AI understood, approve it, and it executes. Upload a vendor invoice PDF — the AI extracts everything and proposes creating the contact, bill, and journal entry atomically.

  • "Who owes me money?" → real AR aging data
