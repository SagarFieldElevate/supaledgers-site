FAQ

Common questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes. 14-day free trial on all plans. No credit card required. You get full access to every feature during the trial.

What does "unlimited users" mean?

It means exactly what it says. On the Professional plan, you can add as many team members, technicians, office staff, and managers as you need. No per-seat fees, no per-tech fees, no hidden charges.

Can I migrate from QuickBooks?

Yes. We provide a migration tool that imports your chart of accounts, contacts, and open invoices/bills from QuickBooks. Enterprise plans include assisted migration with our team.

How does the AI assistant work?

Voice or text commands. You tell SupaLedger what to do in plain English, it shows you what it plans to do, and waits for your explicit approval before executing anything. Every AI action is logged with a full audit trail.

What payment processing fees apply?

Standard Stripe processing fees apply (2.9% + 30¢ per transaction). SupaLedger adds a 0.5% platform fee — lower than most competitors. No monthly gateway fees.

Is my financial data secure?

Bank-level encryption. SOC 2 Type II compliance in progress. Row-level security. Immutable audit logs. Your financial data is treated with the same security standards as a banking application.
