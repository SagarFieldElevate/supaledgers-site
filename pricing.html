FAQ

Common questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes. We're currently in beta and offering free access to the full platform. Join the waitlist and if you're selected, we'll email you with access. No credit card required.

What does "unlimited users" mean?

It means exactly what it says. On the Professional plan, you can add as many team members, technicians, office staff, and managers as you need. No per-seat fees, no per-tech fees, no hidden charges.

Can I migrate from QuickBooks?

Yes. We provide a migration tool that imports your chart of accounts, contacts, and open invoices/bills from QuickBooks. Enterprise plans include assisted migration with our team.

How does the AI assistant work?

Voice or text commands. You tell SupaLedgers what to do in plain English, it shows you what it plans to do, and waits for your explicit approval before executing anything. Every AI action is logged with a full audit trail.

What payment processing fees apply?

Standard Stripe processing fees apply (2.9% + 30¢ per transaction). SupaLedgers adds a 0.5% platform fee — lower than most competitors. No monthly gateway fees.

Can you build custom features for my business?

Yes. Every business operates differently. On Enterprise plans, we build custom features tailored to your specific workflows, industry requirements, and operational needs. Whether it's a custom report, a unique scheduling rule, or a specialized integration — we build it into the platform so it works natively, not as a bolt-on.

Is my financial data secure?

Bank-level encryption. SOC 2 Type II compliance in progress. Row-level security. Immutable audit logs. Your financial data is treated with the same security standards as a banking application.
