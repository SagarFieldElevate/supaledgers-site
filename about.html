About SupaLedgers

Built by people who understand small business.

We've watched small businesses struggle with fragmented software for too long. SupaLedgers exists because we believe there's a better way.

Our Thesis

Every small business deserves enterprise-grade software without enterprise-grade complexity or cost.

The software industry has failed small businesses. Not by ignoring them — there are thousands of tools. But by fragmenting the problem into a dozen separate products that each solve one piece and leave the owner to be the systems integrator.

A plumber shouldn't need to learn QuickBooks sync rules. An HVAC company shouldn't pay $500/tech/month for software. A salon owner shouldn't reconcile two databases every weekend.

We built SupaLedgers on a single, radical principle: one database. When a technician completes a job, the invoice generates and the journal entry posts — in the same transaction. No sync. No reconciliation. No duplicate data. The books are always right because there's nothing to get out of sync.

Single Source of Truth

One database for everything. No sync layers, no reconciliation, no duplicate data. When something changes, it changes everywhere.

Trust Through Transparency

The AI proposes, you approve. Every action has an audit trail. Immutable ledger. Your books, your control.

Fair Pricing

No per-user fees. No hidden charges. A solo plumber and a 25-tech HVAC company get the same price. Period.
