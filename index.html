Everything You Need

Six products. One platform.

Every feature built on a single database. No integrations. No sync layers. Just software that works.

Accounting

Production-grade double-entry ledger. Invoicing, bills, check writing, bank imports with AI categorization. P&L, balance sheet, aging reports — the real deal.

Scheduling

Day and week views with team columns. Drag-and-drop scheduling. Recurring visits. Skill-based routing. Your calendar, built for service businesses.

Dispatch

Visual dispatch board. Create, assign, and dispatch jobs. Mobile tech view with big status buttons and tap-to-navigate. Job lifecycle from start to auto-invoice.

CRM

Contact management with company hierarchy and full-text search. Financial rollup per contact — total invoiced, total paid, outstanding. Know your customers.

AI Assistant

Talk to your books. Voice commands that create invoices, enter bills, categorize transactions, and extract data from PDFs. Every action requires your approval.

Payments

Stripe Connect integration. Branded "View & Pay" emails. Online payment portal. Payment receipts and tracking. Get paid faster, automatically.
